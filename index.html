Welcome

Prof. M. P. Biswal is a senior professor in the Department of Mathematics, IIT Kharagpur. He joined the institute in 1988. He has served as the head of the department from 2016-2019. He completed his B.Sc and M.Sc from Utkal University, Bhubaneshwar and PhD from IIT Kharagpur in 1987.

Research Interests

  • Operations Research
  • Computational Statistics & Stochastic Programming
  • Fuzzy and Convex Optimization
  • Game Theory and Applications
  • Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP)
  • Interior Point Methods (IPM)
  • Multi-Objective Multi-Level & Multi-Choice Programming
